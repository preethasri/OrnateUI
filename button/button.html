Button
Button are used for taking decisions with a single click, like submitting the form.
Contained button
Contained buttons are high-emphasis, distinguished by their use of elevation and fill. They contain actions that are primary to your app.
Outline button
Outlined buttons are medium-emphasis buttons. They contain actions that are important, but aren't the primary action in an app.
Icon button
An icon button is an icon that triggers some sort of action on the page.
Float button
A floating action button appears in front of all screen content, typically as a circular shape with an icon in its center.
